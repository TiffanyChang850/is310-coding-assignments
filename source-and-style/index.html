Welcome!

Social media is a digital object that represents a continuously evolving archive of human communication, identity, and culture. Platforms such as Instagram, TikTok, X (Twitter), and Facebook allow users to create, share, and interact with content in real time, producing massive quantities of digital traces, including posts, images, videos, comments, and metadata.

As a digital object, social media functions both as a tool for social interaction and as a historical record of everyday life. It documents personal experiences, social movements, political discourse, and cultural trends, making it a valuable source for studying contemporary society. Unlike traditional archives, social media content is dynamic, algorithmically curated, and shaped by platform policies and user behavior.
