Link to robots.txt: https://onepiece.fandom.com/robots.txt

#### Why One Piece? ####
One Piece is a pretty popular (and super long) anime. I chose this fandom to webscrape because recently, One Piece started updating their live adaption of the anime, and I watched quite a bit with my brother during spring break. The live adaptation reignited my interest in this anime, which is why I chose to web scrape its wiki page.

#### My focus for this web scrape ####
I want to focus on the character Zoro from the anime. He is one of the main characters and one of the more popular ones as well. I want to scrape for information on his abilities and powers because, in research, it could be clean data that could potentially represent his strength.
